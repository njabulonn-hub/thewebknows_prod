About The Web Knows

Last updated: November 11, 2025 · Author: The Web Knows team

Your Digital Footprint, Revealed Instantly

The Web Knows is an open diagnostics tool that shows you, in real-time, the exact profile of information your browser shares with every website you visit. No installation, no sign-up, and no hidden data collection on our part. It's a window into your own digital exposure.

To ensure this tool remains free and accessible, we now support it with carefully selected advertising. These partners may use their own cookies and tracking under their respective privacy policies.

Our Mission: Demystifying Digital Privacy

Most of us browse the web without realizing how much information we're passively broadcasting. Your device, location, and software create a "digital fingerprint" that can be used to identify and track you. The Web Knows exists to make this invisible data flow visible and understandable, empowering you to take control of your privacy.

Who Is This For?

For Everyday Users: If terms like "User-Agent String" or "IP Geolocation" sound like jargon, you're in the right place. We provide plain-language summaries, real-world examples, and actionable tips to help you understand what's being revealed and why it matters. We translate technical data into insights you can use.

For Professionals (IT, Security, Marketing): Go beyond the basics. Our diagnostic cards highlight potential security risks, data compliance issues (like GDPR or CCPA), and adversarial abuse paths. Use them as a rapid-assessment tool for client audits, employee training, or verifying the effectiveness of your own privacy controls.

How the Diagnostic Works

When you run a scan on The Web Knows, here's what happens in your browser:

  1. Network Discovery: Your browser makes requests to external services to determine your public IP address and infer your approximate geographical location.
  2. Browser & System Profiling: We catalog the information your browser automatically shares, such as your device type, operating system, screen resolution, installed fonts, language preferences, and active browser extensions.
  3. Local Analysis & Display: All this data is processed and displayed directly in your browser. The entire diagnostic logic executes locally on your machine.

A Note on Advertising: Advertising scripts load in parallel with the diagnostic scan. These are separate from our tool and may perform their own measurement or personalization, governed by the privacy controls you have with those specific partners.

Our Data Practices & Advertising Support

We are committed to a model that is both transparent and sustainable.

  • Your Data Stays With You: The results of your diagnostic scan are generated and displayed entirely within your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal scan data to our servers.
  • Minimal First-Party Tracking: We forego separate first-party analytics scripts. The essential reporting we receive comes from our advertising partners to manage billing and ad performance.
  • Transparent Advertising: The ad units you see are served by external networks. These partners may set cookies, use tracking pixels, or access standard browser signals to deliver and measure ads. You can manage these interactions through your browser settings or the partners' own privacy centers.
  • You Are In Control: Feel free to use a VPN, ad blocker, or private browsing mode while using our tool. This will directly demonstrate how these technologies alter the data you present to the web—and may change the scan results and ads you see.
